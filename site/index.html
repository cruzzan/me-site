About me

I am a software developer from western Sweden. I started my career working with my cousin in 2011, and have since then developed a love for working with developing web based SaaS platforms. I like to think about the work that I do in terms of how they relate to the larger context that I am contributing to. I feel that my best work is done when I have a good understanding of who I am building things for, and what they are trying to achieve. As a consequence I am also a strong proponent for helping out wherever it is most needed.

I enjoy running, cooking, hiking, fishing and camping. I love to ski downhill and play golf. I have also been exploring my interest in rock climbing, surfing and sailing. In 2018 I got a skipper certification.

I grew up in a very small town not too far from Gothenburg and spent a few of my teenage years in Melbourne, Australia. After high school I got my first job as a software engineer but after a year I decided that I should probably get an education. So I moved to Karlskrona to study. When I was finished there I moved to Gothenburg to get started with my career.

  • 2019.11 - Founding of West Coast Dev

    At the end of the summer in 2019 Smspark employees were informed that the company had been acquired, after a long process of trying to sell. The new owners were one of our biggest competitors, Easypark. For a number of reasons, this announcement did not land as well as it could have. This led to large portions of the tech-staff, including myself, to leaving our notice of resignation. Almost all the resigning staff were relieved of their duties on the day that they resigned, I was one of the exceptions.

    During my 3-month notice period, I got wind that Easypark would be searching for consultants that could keep the platform running. That is where another colleague, who was still around, decided to float the idea of us taking on the challenge. Instead of getting a third party in, who would need to be brought up to speed.

    This is where I founded West Coast Dev, and began my career as a consultant.
