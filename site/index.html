About me

I am a generally happy and positive guy born in Sweden back in '92. I was raised in the countryside and have always enjoyed the peace and freedom of being close to nature.

I enjoy hiking, kayaking, fishing and camping (All at a *very* novice level). I love to ski downhill. I play golf, rather poorly but still. And in 2018 I got a skipper certification along with some family, just for fun.

I went to school in a very small town not too far from Gothenburg. Apart from year 7-9 which i spent in Melbourne, Australia. After high school I got my first job as a software engineer but after a year I decided that I should probably get an education. So I moved to Karlskrona to study. When I was finished there I moved to Gothenburg to get started with my career.
