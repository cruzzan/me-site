About me

I am a software developer from western Sweden. I started my career working with my cousin in 2011 and have since then developed a love for developing web-based SaaS platforms. I like to think about the work that I do in terms of how it relates to the larger context that I am contributing to. I feel that my best work is done when I have a good understanding of who I am building things for and what they are trying to achieve. As a consequence, I am also a strong proponent for helping out wherever it is most needed.

I enjoy running, cooking, hiking, fishing, and camping. I love to ski downhill and play golf. I have also been exploring my interest in rock climbing, surfing, and sailing. In 2018, I got a skipper certification.

I grew up in a very small town not too far from Gothenburg and spent a few of my teenage years in Melbourne, Australia. After high school, I got my first job as a software engineer, but after a year, I decided that I should probably get an education. So I moved to Karlskrona to study. When I was finished there, I moved to Gothenburg to get started with my career.
